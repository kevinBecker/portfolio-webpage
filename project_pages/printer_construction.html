3D Printer Construction and Maintenance

I have extensive experience in building, maintaining, and optimizing various 3D printers. Here's a chronological overview of my 3D printing journey:

  1. Robo 3D (2014)
  2. Robo 3D R1 (2015)
  3. Prusa i3 MK2s kit (2016)
  4. Prusa i3 MK3 kit (2017) - Currently maintaining 6 units at the MIT Project Manus makerspace (as of 2025)
  5. Prusa i3 MK3s+ MMU2 kit (2018) - Capable of printing in up to 5 materials.
  6. Voron V0.1 (2022) - Custom build using LDO-sourced components
  7. Prusa XL (2025)
