About Me

I am a multidisciplinary engineer with a passion for automation and robotics. My skills include control theory, automation, CAD, robotics, and data analysis.



Education

I received a B.S. from Webb Institute in Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering. My undergraduate senior thesis involved improving waypoint navigation and user interface of an unmanned surface vehicle. Read about it on the projects page!

I then got my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from MIT, working as a graduate research assistant in the Marine Autonomy Lab, focusing on autonomy, control, and simulation.
