COURSE PROJECTS

Nonlinear Controls: Development of an adaptive controller for a dual-thruster surface vehicle. This used a 2nd degree Taylor series expanded model with select cross-coupling terms. A PD and a Sliding Mode controller were implemented with a similar adaptation law. Watch a clip of the PD controller here! View my final project paper here!

Principles of autonomy and Decision Making: 2 person project where we developed a kitchen assistant in simulation utilizing PDDL for high-level planning and an RRT for motion planning of the 7 DOF arm. Watch the video here!

Underactuated Robotics: 3 person project implementing a system of three drones in simulation carrying a suspended load. Utilizing a mathematical solver to compute differential flatness to increase flexibility without re-deriving the equations of motion in the large state-space. Watch the simulated drones follow their trajectories here!

Machine Design: As part of a 6 person project group, we designed and built a CNC desktop lathe capable of cutting steel and aluminum.
